Content direction:

1. Keep Hindu / Indian grounding respectful and light.
2. Keep answers humble; avoid rude or mocking wrong answers.
3. Prefer themes like:
   - dharma
   - satya
   - seva
   - gratitude
   - respect
   - humility
   - courage
   - wise guidance
4. For Ramayana and Mahabharata, focus on key people, places, episodes, and dharmic themes.
5. Do not blindly copy from Wikipedia; use simple curated wording for kids.
